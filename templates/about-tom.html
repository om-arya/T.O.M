Meet T.O.M.

Powered by Google’s Gemini API through the Google AI Studio, the Tabby of Motivation or T.O.M., is an AI-powered web app that provides helpful support and encouragement to college students (like you!) as they navigate the stressful ins and outs of managing their academic lives.

Hear His Wisdom

Having been around for a long time (cats have nine lives after all), T.O.M. has quite the wisdom to share! On each click, T.O.M. will share a spontaneous piece of his unique insight into the art of pursuing your dreams and never giving up to keep your claws sharp.

Plan Your Day Out

Burdened by too many things to get done in one day, and not knowing where to get started? Share your worries with T.O.M., and receive a personalized schedule to get your work done. If you want a new schedule, simply ask T.O.M. again!

Study With T.O.M.

Struggling to focus on your studies for a big exam tomorrow? Time your studies and accompanying breaks with this customizable study timer, featuring plenty of color combinations for a brand new look every time. You can even ask T.O.M. to make you a theme for your mood at the moment!

Something to Tell Us? We're Listening!

Whether it’s wanting a new way to hang out with T.O.M. or finding a hairball that’s causing him to act like a grumpy cat, we’re here to address any concerns you may have! Contact us below and we will unwind any yarn balls tangled T.O.M. may be caught in to get him working again.
